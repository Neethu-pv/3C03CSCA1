Rivers In Kasaragod

Kasaragod is the one of the most beautiful districts in Kerala gifted with twelve rivers out of a total of 44 rivers that flows in Kerala.The longest riverof Kasaragod is Chandragiri which is starting from Pattimala in Coorg and embraces the sea at Thalangara,near Kasaragod-Kerala.The river believes its name Chandragiri from the name of the place of its source Chandragupta Vasti;where the great Maurya emperor Chandragupta is believed to have spent his last days as a sage.
